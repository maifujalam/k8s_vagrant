23. Cipher Suite: https://github.com/kodekloudhub/certified-kubernetes-security-specialist-cks-course/blob/main/docs/03-Cluster-Setup-and-Hardening/49-Cipher-Suites.md
    Summery:-
    a. In kube-apiserver,kube-controller-manager,kube-scheduler:-
        --tls-cipher-suites: XX,YY and --tls-min-version VersionTLS12  [ kube-apiserver tls cipher suite]
    b.In etcd /etc/kubernetes/manifests/etcd.yaml:
        --cipher-suites: XX,YY
    c.In kubelet /var/lib/kubelet/config.yaml:
        tlsCipherSuites: [TLS_AES_128_GCM_SHA256, TLS_ECDHE_ECDSA_WITH_AES_128_GCM_SHA256, TLS_ECDHE_ECDSA_WITH_AES_256_CBC_SHA]
        tlsMinVersion: VersionTLS11
24 For app armor:-
    a. Add under pod spec:
        spec:
          securityContext:
            appArmorProfile:
              type: Localhost
              localhostProfile: k8s-apparmor-example-deny-write
    b. Add annotations also:
          annotations:
            container.apparmor.security.beta.kubernetes.io/hello: localhost/k8s-apparmor-example-allow-write
